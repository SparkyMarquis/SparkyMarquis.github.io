About the Artist


Sarah Marquis is a self-trained artist and avid fan of Magic: The Gathering in all forms - art, lore, gameplay and collecting.

She is force to be reckoned with on the Pokemon battlefield, in addition to her formidable art skills.

When not persuing her dreams of being an artist or Pokemon Master, she can usually be found cuddled up with her boyfriend.
